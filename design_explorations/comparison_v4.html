Checklist — Bold Serif Headings, Full-Page Glow

Bomu wordmark stays rounded sans. Page headline switches to a bold condensed high-contrast serif (Asia's 50 Best Bars style). Category labels in small-caps serif. Amber glow now spans the full page instead of just the header.

Variant A
Single-line headline, wide layout
"Mixers & Ingredients" fits on one line at a slightly smaller size, serif is elegant rather than heavy. Uniform ring-lit tiles.
Read: More restrained and legible day-to-day. Tile lighting is a bit uniform/repetitive across all 12 — reads slightly "template" rather than each shot feeling bespoke.
Variant B
Two-line bold masthead, editorial photo tiles
Headline breaks to two lines at real awards-plaque scale and weight. Tile photography is more varied and editorial (whole limes, kumquats, dried orange wheels) rather than uniform product shots, background is a soft out-of-focus bar photo instead of flat gradient.
Read: My pick. This is the one that actually looks like the Asia's 50 Best reference — bigger, bolder, more confident serif, and the varied ingredient photography feels premium rather than templated. The out-of-focus bar backdrop is a nice touch that ties the whole page together.
